Return Policy

30-Day Returns

We offer a 30-day return policy on most items. Items must be unused and in original packaging.

How to Return an Item

  1. Log into your account
  2. Go to Order History
  3. Select the item you wish to return
  4. Follow the return instructions
  5. Print the return shipping label
  6. Ship the item back to us

Return Conditions

  • Items must be returned within 30 days of delivery
  • Items must be in original condition with all tags and packaging
  • Some items are final sale and cannot be returned
  • Return shipping costs may apply

Refund Processing

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive your return. Refunds will be issued to your original payment method.

Need Help?

Contact our support team at returns@arolana.com or call 1-800-AROLANA
